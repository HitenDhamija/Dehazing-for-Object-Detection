Dataset: RTTS (Real Task-Driven Test Set)
Input: Hazy images
Detection Model: YOLOv11m (Ultralytics pretrained)

Baseline Experiment: Detection on hazy images (no dehazing)

Observations:

* Multiple persons detected successfully
* Some objects detected with low confidence due to haze
* Partial occlusion and reduced clarity observed
* Possible missed detections in dense haze regions
* Bounding boxes sometimes less precise

Conclusion:
Haze negatively affects object detection performance.
